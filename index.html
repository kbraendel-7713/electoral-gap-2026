THE ELECTORAL GAP

Six states. Nine House districts. One unoccupied margin.

The margin of every close race is full of voters both parties ignore. We have their names, the contradictions that move them, and a field-tested $0.12-per-person machine to reach them. Half a million of them sit in nine districts that decide the 2026 House.

The Gap

30.4M

Reinforcement voters — conditional, issue-responsive, unactivated

Every competitive campaign runs the same two plays: persuade high-turnout swing voters, mobilize the base. Both plays are correct — and fully priced in. What remains is the actual margin of the 2026 House: mid- and low-turnout voters whose participation is conditional and issue-driven — 27.5 million reachable from a Republican perspective. Too unlikely to vote for GOTV budgets. Too mixed on the ballot for safe persuasion. So nobody talks to them at all.
